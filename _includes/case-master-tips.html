Tips

Last updated: May 13, 2022

This Google Sheets Add-on helps you to change the case of any text into many possible options. The following tips will help you to get the maximum out of the Add-on.

  • After installing the Add-on, it will show up under the Extensions menu of Google Sheets
  • Search for Case Master under this menu
  • If you mouse over the Case Master, it will display all possible case change options divided into three different sections
  • Users can select cells via keyboard or mouse click
  • At the bottom section, users can click on Activate or Get License to get the premium version to get full access to Add-on
  • If you want to use the free version, you can always change the case of 100 rows and 1 column at once
  • The paid version allows you to change the CASE of unlimited cells
